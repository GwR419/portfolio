This Game

Setting

It seems you are in a cabin in the middle of nowhere with your child, The man, and yourself the only reason we know this is because the man tells us where we are at while all we see is the table and the card game. (In the future versions of this game Inscryption we can walk around the cabin and solve puzzles.) What makes this setting distinctive is that it is all surrounded by mystery. We have an idea of what is around us through words and storytelling but other than that we are clueless.

What kind(s) of character(s)

How are you challenged

The player is challenged in the way of a card game where you must think ahead and make the correct sacrifices to win the card game. With each level it gets increasingly difficult, but when you die you get to start the round over again so not to much punishment for losing but you can get stuck easily based off how you play.
